About Us

Career Guidance System is an intelligent platform designed to help students discover suitable career paths using machine learning. The system analyzes key factors such as academic performance, technical skills and personal interests to generate personalized career predictions. By using data-driven insights, the platform helps students understand their strengths and explore career opportunities that align with their abilities. In addition to prediction, it also provides skill analysis and guidance to support better career planning. The goal of this platform is to simplify the career decision-making process and help students choose a path that matches their potential. Through the use of modern technologies and intelligent analysis, the system aims to guide students toward more confident and informed career choices.
