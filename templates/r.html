R Programming

Introduction

R is a powerful programming language and environment used for statistical computing, data analysis, and data visualization. It is widely used by data scientists, statisticians, and researchers for analyzing large datasets and building predictive models.

Why Should You Learn R?

  • Best language for statistical analysis and data science.
  • Strong visualization libraries like ggplot2.
  • Widely used in research and academia.
  • Large collection of packages for machine learning and analytics.
  • High demand in data-driven industries.

Job Roles

  • Data Analyst
  • Data Scientist
  • Statistician
  • Research Analyst
  • Business Intelligence Analyst

Career Opportunities

R professionals are highly valued in data science, healthcare analytics, financial modeling, and academic research. Companies and research institutions rely on R for statistical modeling and predictive analytics. Learning R opens strong career opportunities in data science and analytics domains.
