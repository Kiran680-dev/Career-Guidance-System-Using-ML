Prolog

Introduction

Prolog is a logic programming language associated with artificial intelligence and computational linguistics. Unlike traditional programming languages, Prolog is based on formal logic and uses facts and rules to derive conclusions. It is widely used in AI systems, expert systems, and knowledge-based applications.

Why Should You Learn Prolog?

  • Strong foundation in logic programming.
  • Widely used in artificial intelligence research.
  • Ideal for building expert systems.
  • Enhances problem-solving and reasoning skills.
  • Useful in computational linguistics and symbolic AI.

Job Roles

  • AI Developer
  • Research Programmer
  • Logic Programmer
  • Machine Learning Researcher
  • Software Engineer (AI Systems)

Career Opportunities

Prolog is commonly used in artificial intelligence research, academic institutions, and expert system development. It is valuable for careers in AI, knowledge representation, and advanced reasoning systems. Learning Prolog strengthens logical thinking and opens research-oriented career paths.
